# Hat-in-Ring Radar — Overview

**Who's moving toward 2028 — and how seriously.**

Hat-in-Ring Radar is a live dashboard that tracks every potential 2028 presidential candidate on two things that usually get blurred together:

- **How far they've actually gone** — FEC filings, exploratory committees, on-record "I'm considering it" quotes.
- **How much momentum they have right now** — early-state travel, donors, staffing, and media, weighted and decaying over time.

It's rebuilt every day from **FEC filings and the news wire**, with every signal sourced and every score explained. Front-runners who are publicly downplaying a run sit low on status but high on momentum — because **status isn't support**, and the board shows both.

**It is not a rumor board.** Unverified names go to a review queue, explicit denials are never auto-applied, and money is tracked as its own axis — never baked into the hype.

See the seriousness leaderboard, momentum trajectories, early-state heat, head-to-head comparisons, and a daily "what moved" briefing at **[hatinring.com](https://hatinring.com)**.
